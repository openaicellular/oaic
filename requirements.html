Hardware and Software Requirements

Hardware Requirements:

Integrated O-RAN Solution

O-RAN system:

  • 1 computer with 10 Gbps NIC

  • 1 SDR (X or N for 5G)

Each UE:

  • 1 computer with 10 Gbps NIC

  • 1 SDR (X or N for 5G)

    • e.g. X310 or N310 USRP
